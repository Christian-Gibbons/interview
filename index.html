Job Interview

  • High Concept: A job interview where you select your answers to questions.
  • Player Experience Goals: Explore the dialog options and see where they lead.
  • Genre: An interview simulator.
  • Game play: The player uses the mouse to click on what they wish to say.
  • Prototype goal: Work in mouse-based controls, a branching conversation, and voice-acting.
  • Features: It has voiced dialog
  • Setting/Premise: You are at a job interview. Don't screw it up.
  • Target Audience: No particular audience, just working in some new techniques that may be useful in the future possibly for a menu system and an RPG with dialog opions.
  • Play time: a couple minutes to see one dialog path.
  • Strategies: Try not to say something stupid.
  • Prototype Assets: Voice Acting and Story by Grant Gibbons.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Various internet quizzes.
